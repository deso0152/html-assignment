About me

It’s all good in the woods. I love being outdoors, especially when I have friends and a camera with me. Even though I love being a product photographer, I also love photographing nature. So much that I even created a website to sell my prints. It is called Alves Fine Art.

At a first glance, regarding my personality, you might think I’m introverted but don’t be fooled, the more I get comfortable with those around me, the more I can get you laughing, by me or of me. I’m always open to new experiences, opportunities to meet new people are valuable to me. So what is left to say is, my name is Paulo Alves and I`m looking forward to meeting you.
